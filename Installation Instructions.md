Thanks for downloading Armory App! Below are some quick start guides on how to get it working on your device.
If you have any issues with getting it running, do not hesitate to ping me on discord, or DM me directly.

__ ANDROID __

Open Armory App.apk file

Google Play Protect will prompt you to scan the file. This is because the app is not signed yet through Google. 
You can press More Details > Install without scanning OR Scan App. It does not matter which you choose, but install without scanning is faster.

__ iOS __

iOS is much more involved than Android. It's gotten rather simple, but here's a quick start guide for AltStore, a popular choice of sideloader.

Plug your device into your computer.

Install AltServer from altstore.io for Windows or MacOS (Scroll to the bottom of the page)

Install iCloud and iTunes from the Apple website. Do not use the store versions as they do not work.

Open iTunes, and be sure that your device can sync over wifi.

Open AltServer (usually in the bottom right of your taskbar) and select install AltStore. This will prompt you for your Apple id and password.
This information is sent directly to Apple to generate a signing certificate for your apps, basically a key that allows them to be installed and ran.

Once the installer has finished, you will see the app on your home page. We're not done yet.

Go to your settings > General > VPN and Device Management, tap on your Apple id, and tap trust. Now, the app will open.

If this setting is unavailable or missing, go to settings > Privacy and Security and turn Developer Mode ON—your phone will restart.

Finally, open AltStore, and tap on the plus + icon. Select armoryapp.ipa, and let it install.



You're finally good to go. Enjoy!
